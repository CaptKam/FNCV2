# FORK & COMPASS: MASTER BLUEPRINT (v1.1)
*Pick a country, cook a dinner, feel like you traveled.*
**Confidential · April 2026**

## 1. Product Vision
Fork & Compass is a premium culinary travel iOS app. It targets serious home cooks who want cultural travel experiences through food. The app provides a complete end-to-end cooking experience from discovery to the table.

## 2. Design Philosophy
Every pixel is governed by three frameworks: Jobsian functional aesthetics, Apple’s HIG, and luxury editorial sensibility.

### 2.4 — 2026 Spatial Design & Liquid Glass
Embraces "Liquid Glass"—a translucent, dynamic material system. Treat the interface in 3D space (Z-depth).
*   **Z-0 is Content:** Full-bleed photography and mesh gradients.
*   **Z-1 is Glass:** Navigation and modal sheets using OS blur materials.
*   **Dynamic Response:** Materials adapt to content (e.g., shrinking tab bar).
*   **Specular Highlights:** 1px semi-transparent white top-border on glass edges.

## 3. Design System
### 3.1 — Semantic Color & Material Architecture
| Token | Light Mode | Dark Mode | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Surface.Base** | #FEF9F3 | #121212 | Minimal usage. |
| **Surface.Glass** | UltraThinMaterialLight | UltraThinMaterialDark | Tab bars, headers, cards. |
| **Brand.Primary** | #9A4100 | #C85A17 | Active states, CTAs. |
| **Text.Hero** | #1A1A1A | #FFFFFF | Primary titles. |
| **Text.Editorial** | #4A4A4A | #E0E0E0 | Descriptions, context. |

### 3.2 — Typography
*   **Headlines:** Noto Serif 700 Bold
*   **Body:** Inter 400 Regular
*   **Emphasis:** Inter 500 Medium
*   **Editorial:** Noto Serif 500 Medium

### 3.3 — Spacing, Radius, and Edges
*   **radius-sm:** 8pt (badges)
*   **radius-md:** 16pt (glass cards)
*   **radius-lg:** 24pt (modal sheets)
*   **radius-pill:** 9999pt (tab bar, buttons)

## 4. Navigation Architecture
Five-tab architecture using a dynamic "Liquid Tab" that floats above content:
1. **Discover:** Inspiration & exploration.
2. **Search:** Targeted recipe finding.
3. **Plan:** Weekly prep & parties.
4. **Grocery:** Assemble ingredients.
5. **Cook:** Flawless meal execution.
